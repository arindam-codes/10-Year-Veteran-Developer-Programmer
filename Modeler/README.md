**3D Modeling in Roblox is actually the biggest and most diverse role.**

It splits into **8 different specializations**.

Most modelers specialize in **one or two of them**.

Below is the **complete Roblox Modeler Skill Tree analysed from the market posts.**

---

# Roblox 3D Modeler Skill Tree (Market Analysis)

## 1. Core Modeling Fundamentals (Foundation)

Every modeling job requires these.

### Geometry Basics

* Vertices
* Edges
* Faces
* Mesh topology

### Polygon Control

* Low poly
* Mid poly
* High poly

Example from posts:

> "optimized for Roblox"

This means:

* low triangle count
* efficient meshes
* good topology

---

# 2. Blender Skills (Main Tool)

Most Roblox modelers use **Blender**.

Required skills:

* hard surface modeling
* sculpting basics
* modifiers
* bevel
* mirror
* array
* boolean

Also required:

* exporting **FBX files**
* scale correction for Roblox

---

# 14. Optimization (VERY IMPORTANT)

Roblox has strict limits.

Skills required:

* triangle count control

* efficient meshes

# 15. UV Mapping

Required for:

* textures
* materials
* decals

# 16. Style Matching

* cartoon

### Horror

# 17. Reference-Based Modeling

Very common.

Example from posts:

> "match reference image"

Developers provide concept art.

Your job is to **replicate it in 3D**.

# 18. Modular Asset Creation

Professional modeling technique.

Example:

Instead of building one house…

You create:

* wall pieces
* door pieces
* roof pieces

Then the builder assembles them.

→ scaling
→ collision setup
